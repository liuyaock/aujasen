About us

Production of diving equipment

Aujasen Diving Equipment Mfy Co., Ltd is an experienced manufactory of scuba diving equipment that located in Zhanjiang City, Guangdong Province, China.As a qualify supplier in diving equipment manufacturing for more than 30 years, we has provided outstanding diving products to scores of countries around the world.

We moved to a new, big factory and warehouse in the end of 2016. This enables Aujasen to have a large manufacture capacity and prompter lead time to satisfy more customers, and to better our long term relationship of mutual benefit with customers.Our brand name “Aujasen” has gained its reputation in domestic market with stable quality and competitive price.
